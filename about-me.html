My name is Alison Mae Bonham. I was born in Houston, Texas at 24 weeks. I weighed just under 2 lbs. In 2008, I moved with my family to Austin, Texas, where I discovered my love for photography. By December of 2009, I had started my portrait photography business, Mae Photography,(now Alison Mae Photography) . I carried my love for business and photography all the way to sunny California, where I attended Brooks Institute of Photography. I lived in almost every town and city down the coast from Santa Barbara to Ventura, and eventually, Los Angeles, CA, where I spent the last year living and working in freelance photography, social media marketing, and basically any film or commercial gig I could get my hands on.

I'm beyond excited for my future in code - What's next?
